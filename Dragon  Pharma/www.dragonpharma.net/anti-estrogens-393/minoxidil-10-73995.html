What is Minoxidil?

Minoxidil was originally developed to treat high blood pressure, but it was soon discovered to significantly promote hair growth. This vasodilator increases blood flow to hair follicles, helping nourish and reactivate them — especially those damaged by genetic baldness. Oral Minoxidil is now widely used off-label for hair restoration, and Dragon Pharma Minoxidil is among the most potent options available.

How Does Minoxidil Work?

Minoxidil widens blood vessels, improving circulation to hair follicles. This enhanced nutrient and oxygen delivery helps extend the anagen (growth) phase of the hair cycle. The result: stronger follicles, improved density, and new visible hair growth over time.

Recommended Dosage

Typical dosage is 10 mg (1 tablet) per day, taken with a full glass of water. It can be taken with or without food, but consistency is key. Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations for best results and to minimize side effects.

Minoxidil Cycle Example (3–6 Months)

  • Months 1–3: Take 1 tablet daily. Monitor shedding and scalp response.
  • Months 4–6: Continue daily use. Evaluate hair density and growth.

Improvements are typically seen within 3–6 months of consistent use. Stopping may result in gradual reversal of benefits.

Potential Side Effects

  • Temporary increased shedding (initial weeks)
  • Scalp dryness or irritation
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness (due to vasodilation)
  • Rare swelling of hands/feet or fluid retention

If you experience chest pain, fast heartbeat, or severe dizziness, stop use and seek medical advice immediately.
