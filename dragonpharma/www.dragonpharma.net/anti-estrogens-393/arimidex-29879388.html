What is Arimidex?

Arimidex (Anastrozole) is a third-generation aromatase inhibitor designed to block estrogen production by inhibiting the aromatase enzyme. Originally developed for hormone receptor-positive breast cancer in women, it has become a key component of post-cycle therapy (PCT) for male bodybuilders using aromatizing anabolic steroids such as Testosterone Enanthate and Sustanon.

How Does Arimidex Work?

By suppressing estrogen levels, Arimidex helps prevent estrogenic side effects like water retention, gynecomastia (male breast tissue), and bloating. A typical bodybuilding dose is 0.5 to 1 mg daily or every other day, depending on steroid cycle intensity and individual estrogen sensitivity.

Benefits of Using Arimidex

  • Prevents gynecomastia during steroid cycles
  • Reduces bloating and water retention
  • Supports testosterone optimization post-cycle
  • Helps maintain a lean, dry physique

Potential Side Effects

Although Arimidex is well-tolerated, high doses or misuse can cause joint pain, fatigue, and mood changes due to estrogen depletion. Always use responsibly and consult a healthcare professional if needed.

Does Arimidex Require PCT?

Arimidex is often used during a cycle to manage estrogen levels, not after. For post-cycle recovery, Clomid or Nolvadex are more appropriate to restore natural testosterone production.
