What is LGD 4033?

LGD 4033, also known as Ligandrol, is a selective androgen receptor modulator (SARM) developed to treat conditions like muscle wasting and osteoporosis. In bodybuilding, it's widely used for its ability to increase lean muscle mass, improve recovery time, and enhance strength – without the harsh androgenic side effects of traditional steroids.

Ligandrol binds selectively to androgen receptors in muscle and bone tissue, promoting anabolic activity while minimizing unwanted effects on the prostate, skin, or organs.

How to Use LGD 4033

Most users start with 5 mg/day and gradually increase to 10 mg/day based on individual response. A typical cycle lasts 8–12 weeks. Because of its 24–36 hour half-life, LGD 4033 is taken once per day. It stacks well with other SARMs like RAD 140 or Ostarine (MK-2866) for strength and cutting phases.

Side Effects & Safety

LGD 4033 is generally well-tolerated. However, minor side effects may include headaches, fatigue, or nausea in rare cases. While it doesn't aromatize, prolonged use may cause mild testosterone suppression – making PCT optional but beneficial.

Post-Cycle Therapy (PCT)

If used longer than 8 weeks or stacked with suppressive compounds, include PCT using Clomid or Nolvadex for 2–4 weeks to help restore natural testosterone production.
