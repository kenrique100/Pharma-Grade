What is Aromasin?

Aromasin, or Exemestane, is a third-generation aromatase inhibitor used in both clinical oncology and performance-enhancing cycles. In bodybuilding, it helps reduce estrogenic side effects caused by aromatizing steroids such as Testosterone Enanthate or Sustanon.

How Does Aromasin Work?

Aromasin works by irreversibly inhibiting the aromatase enzyme, which is responsible for converting androgens into estrogens. It is effective at lowering estrogen levels by up to 85%, making it a powerful choice during or after anabolic steroid cycles to prevent water retention, bloating, and gynecomastia.
