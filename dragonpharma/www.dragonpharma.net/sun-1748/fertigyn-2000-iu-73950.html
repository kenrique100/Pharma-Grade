What Is HCG (Human Chorionic Gonadotropin)?

HCG mimics luteinizing hormone (LH), signaling the testes to produce testosterone naturally. Athletes use it during cycles to prevent testicular shrinkage and maintain fertility, and during post-cycle therapy (PCT) to help restore endogenous hormone production.
