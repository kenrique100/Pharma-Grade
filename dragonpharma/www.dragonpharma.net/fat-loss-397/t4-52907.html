What is T4 (Levothyroxine Sodium)?

Levothyroxine is the prohormone of triiodothyronine (T3), meaning your body converts T4 into the more active T3 form. This conversion process makes T4 a steady and reliable option for stimulating metabolism without the more aggressive side effects of direct T3 supplementation. T4 is widely used for treating hypothyroidism, but it also has applications in fat-loss protocols among bodybuilders and athletes.
