GHRP-2 is considered a more refined peptide, offering similar or even greater GH-releasing potency but with minimal effect on hunger. This makes GHRP-2 the preferred choice for dieting phases or for users who are sensitive to appetite swings.

How should I reconstitute and store GHRP-2?
