British Dragon T3 25 mcg Tablets

T3 25 mcg contains Liothyronine Sodium, a synthetic form of triiodothyronine (T3), one of the body's primary thyroid hormones. T3 plays a critical role in regulating metabolism, energy expenditure, and fat utilization, making it a popular compound among bodybuilders and athletes during cutting and fat-loss phases.

Unlike T4 (thyroxine), which must be converted into T3 by the body, Liothyronine is already in its active form. This allows T3 to act quickly and directly on metabolic processes, increasing basal metabolic rate and accelerating calorie expenditure.

Due to its potency, T3 is considered an advanced metabolic aid and is typically used by experienced users who understand thyroid hormone management. When used responsibly, it can support significant fat loss while helping preserve lean muscle mass when combined with proper nutrition and training.

The physiological role and pharmacology of Liothyronine Sodium are well documented. A detailed clinical overview can be found through the National Library of Medicine (PubMed), which explains how T3 regulates metabolic activity at the cellular level.

Effects

The primary effect of T3 25 mcg is a significant increase in metabolic rate. Users commonly experience enhanced fat loss, increased body temperature, and greater caloric expenditure throughout the day.

T3 promotes faster nutrient turnover, allowing the body to utilize stored fat more efficiently. This makes it especially useful during cutting cycles, contest preparation, or recomposition phases where fat reduction is a primary goal.

When combined with proper diet and resistance training, T3 may help preserve lean muscle tissue while reducing body fat. However, misuse or excessive dosing may increase the risk of muscle breakdown.

Dosage

T3 dosing must be approached cautiously. Typical dosages range from 12.5 mcg to 75 mcg per day, depending on experience level and metabolic tolerance.

Most users begin with a low dose of 12.5–25 mcg per day and gradually increase over time to assess tolerance. Splitting the daily dose into two administrations may help maintain stable blood levels.

Abrupt discontinuation after extended use is not recommended. Gradual tapering allows natural thyroid function to normalize and reduces the risk of post-cycle metabolic slowdown.

Cycle

T3 is most commonly used during cutting cycles focused on fat loss and physique refinement. It is often combined with thermogenic compounds such as Clenbuterol for synergistic metabolic effects.

During more advanced cutting protocols, T3 may be stacked alongside anabolic agents like Stanabol 50 to help preserve lean muscle mass while calories are reduced.

Some athletes also incorporate T3 during recomposition phases with injectable bases such as Primobol Inj, where metabolic support is desired without excessive androgenic strain.

T3 should not be used continuously for long periods and is best reserved for structured cycles with defined start and end points.

Possible Side Effects

When used responsibly, T3 is generally well tolerated. However, excessive dosing or prolonged use may lead to side effects such as increased heart rate, sweating, anxiety, insomnia, and fatigue.

High doses may suppress natural thyroid hormone production temporarily. This is why gradual tapering and conservative cycle planning are essential.

T3 does not exhibit hepatotoxic effects and is considered liver-safe. Monitoring cardiovascular response is recommended, especially at higher doses.
