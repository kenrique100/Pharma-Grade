Selective Estrogen Receptor Modulators (SERMs)

SERMs are a cornerstone of effective post-cycle therapy (PCT), especially after anabolic steroid use. These compounds do not reduce estrogen levels like aromatase inhibitors — instead, they block estrogen at the receptor level, particularly in tissues like the breast. This makes them ideal for reversing testosterone suppression and preventing gynecomastia after a cycle.

Dragon Pharma's SERM lineup includes Clomiphene Citrate (Clomid), Tamoxifen Citrate (Nolvadex), Toremifene, and Raloxifene. These compounds are trusted by bodybuilders, athletes, and TRT patients for stimulating luteinizing hormone (LH) and kickstarting natural testosterone production. SERMs are also used by female athletes in very specific cases under strict protocols.

When to use SERMs: Typically during the 4–6 weeks after a steroid cycle ends. Some users also include low-dose SERMs during a cycle, depending on their stack and sensitivity to estrogen.

Benefits: Improved hormonal balance, faster testosterone recovery, reduced risk of estrogen rebound, and better retention of lean mass gains.
