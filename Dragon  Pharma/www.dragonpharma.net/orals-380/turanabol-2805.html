What Is Turanabol?

Turanabol (also called Turinabol or Tbol) is a modified version of Dianabol, altered to remove aromatization and reduce androgenic side effects. It contains 4-Chlorodehydromethyltestosterone and is commonly used during both cutting and recomposition cycles thanks to its clean profile.

Originally developed in East Germany in the 1960s, it became the go-to anabolic compound for Olympic athletes in the infamous GDR doping programs. Today, it's appreciated for delivering strength, endurance, and definition without water retention.

Benefits of Turanabol (Tbol)

  • No estrogenic side effects or bloating
  • Low androgenic activity – well tolerated by men and women
  • Useful as a bridge between cycles or during contest prep

Tbol does not convert to estrogen, so users experience clean, dry gains. It also reduces SHBG levels, improving the effectiveness of other steroids when stacked.
