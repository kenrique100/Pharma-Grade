Axiolabs Clomiplex 50 mg Tablets

Clomiplex 50 mg contains Clomiphene Citrate, a well-known selective estrogen receptor modulator (SERM) widely used by bodybuilders, athletes, and fitness enthusiasts to support natural testosterone recovery after anabolic steroid cycles. Clomiphene has long been considered a cornerstone compound in structured post-cycle therapy (PCT) protocols.

Clomiphene works by blocking estrogen receptors in the hypothalamus and pituitary gland. This action reduces estrogen's negative feedback on the hypothalamic–pituitary–gonadal axis, leading to increased secretion of luteinizing hormone (LH) and follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH). As a result, the body is stimulated to resume endogenous testosterone production.

Unlike aromatase inhibitors, which lower estrogen production, Clomiplex allows estrogen levels to remain present while preventing estrogen from suppressing hormonal signaling in the brain. This makes it particularly effective for recovery while preserving estrogen's beneficial roles in joint health, mood, and cardiovascular function.

The pharmacology and clinical applications of Clomiphene Citrate are well documented. A detailed scientific overview is available via the National Library of Medicine (PubMed).

Effects

The primary effect of Clomiplex 50 mg is stimulation of natural testosterone production following suppressive cycles. Users typically report improved energy levels, libido recovery, and overall hormonal balance during post-cycle therapy.

By restoring LH and FSH signaling, Clomiplex supports testicular function and helps reduce the duration of post-cycle suppression. Many athletes experience a smoother transition off-cycle when Clomiphene is used correctly.

Clomiplex does not directly affect muscle mass but plays a critical role in preserving gains by promoting hormonal stability during recovery.

Dosage

Typical Clomiplex dosages range from 25 mg to 50 mg per day, depending on cycle length, compound selection, and individual recovery needs.

Many users begin post-cycle therapy with 50 mg daily for the first 1–2 weeks, followed by a reduction to 25 mg daily as recovery progresses. Due to its long half-life, once-daily dosing is sufficient.

Excessively high dosages do not accelerate recovery and may increase the likelihood of side effects. Structured dosing is strongly recommended.

Cycle

Clomiplex is most commonly used during post-cycle therapy following suppressive anabolic steroid cycles. It is often paired with Tamoxiplex to provide comprehensive estrogen receptor management and testosterone recovery support.

After testosterone-based cycles such as Testaplex E 250, Clomiplex is typically introduced once exogenous hormones clear the system, helping restart endogenous testosterone production.

Some advanced users may also combine Clomiplex with compounds like Proviraplex during recovery to support libido and androgenic balance while natural testosterone levels normalize.

Clomiplex is intended for post-cycle use and should not replace aromatase inhibitors during high-estrogen cycles.

Possible Side Effects

Clomiplex is generally well tolerated when used at recommended dosages. Some users may experience mild side effects such as headaches, visual disturbances, or mood fluctuations.

These effects are typically dose-dependent and resolve once dosage is reduced or discontinued. Long-term continuous use is not advised.

Clomiphene does not exhibit significant hepatotoxicity and is considered liver-safe when used responsibly.
