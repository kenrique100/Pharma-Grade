What Is AICAR?

AICAR functions as an AMPK activator, mimicking the effects of exercise at the cellular level. Originally developed for metabolic and ischemic studies, it has been investigated for its effects on energy homeostasis, endurance performance, and glucose uptake. In rodents and primates, AICAR has demonstrated the potential to increase stamina without physical training.

Mechanism of Action

AICAR activates AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK), a key enzyme that signals the body to enhance energy production, increase fatty acid oxidation, and improve glucose transport. This process leads to improved endurance, enhanced insulin sensitivity, and potentially accelerated fat loss.
