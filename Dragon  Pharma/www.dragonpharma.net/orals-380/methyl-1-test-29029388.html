What is Methyl-1-Test 10?

Methyl-1-Testosterone (M1T) is a synthetic derivative of testosterone, designed for extreme anabolic activity. It delivers significant muscle mass and strength gains over short cycles, making it ideal for advanced bulking phases. M1T is more potent than many traditional oral steroids and does not convert to estrogen, reducing the risk of water retention.

This oral steroid is often compared to Superdrol or Halotestin in terms of aggressiveness and strength output, but with a distinct anabolic profile that promotes dense muscle buildup quickly.

How to Use Methyl-1-Test 10

Because of its potency and liver toxicity, M1T cycles are short. Typical dosage ranges from 10–20 mg/day for no more than 2–4 weeks. Beginners may start at 10 mg/day to assess tolerance. M1T is commonly used as a "kickstart" to injectable bulking cycles.

Stacking with a testosterone base like Testosterone Cypionate improves results and minimizes suppression. Always use a liver support supplement during the cycle.

Post-Cycle Therapy (PCT)

After stopping M1T, start your PCT within 2–3 days. A typical PCT stack includes:

  • Clomid: 50 mg/day for 2 weeks
  • Nolvadex (optional): 20 mg/day for 3 weeks

This restores testosterone production and helps retain lean gains. PCT is critical after using M1T due to strong suppression of natural hormones.

Potential Side Effects

M1T is harsh on the liver, so cycle length should be limited. Other side effects may include increased blood pressure, lethargy, joint dryness, acne, and aggression. Not recommended for female users due to virilization risk.

To mitigate risk, monitor liver enzymes, hydrate regularly, and avoid combining with other hepatotoxic compounds.

Warnings

Methyl-1-Test is not suitable for beginners or individuals under 21. Do not exceed recommended dosage. Always cycle off and include proper PCT. Use is at your own risk and should be supervised by a medical or fitness professional.
