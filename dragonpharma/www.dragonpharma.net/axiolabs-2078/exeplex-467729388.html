Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Exeplex 25 different from Arimidex?

Exeplex (Exemestane) is a suicidal AI, meaning it permanently disables the aromatase enzyme. Arimidex is a reversible inhibitor, so its effects are temporary.

Should I use Exeplex 25 during or after a cycle?

You can use it during cycles with aromatizing steroids or after a cycle to prevent estrogen rebound. Dosage may vary based on timing and bloodwork.

Does Exeplex reduce testosterone?

No, it prevents testosterone from converting into estrogen, which can indirectly improve testosterone ratios. It does not suppress natural testosterone on its own.
