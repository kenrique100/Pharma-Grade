What is Tesamorelin?

Tesamorelin is a synthetic peptide analog of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH). It works by stimulating the pituitary gland to increase the body's own production of human growth hormone (HGH), making it especially useful for fat loss, lean muscle gain, and vitality enhancement.

Originally studied for HIV-related lipodystrophy, Tesamorelin has gained attention among athletes and performance-focused individuals due to its ability to improve body composition, reduce visceral fat, and boost recovery.
