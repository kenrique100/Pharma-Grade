What Is Proviron?

Proviron (Mesterolone) is an oral dihydrotestosterone (DHT) derivative. It acts as a mild androgen and SHBG-binding agent, often used to boost free testosterone levels, improve libido, enhance muscle definition, and mitigate estrogen-related issues during anabolic steroid cycles.

Unlike aromatase inhibitors such as Arimidex, Proviron does not reduce estrogen via aromatase inhibition. Instead, it blocks estrogen receptor activity in tissues and frees up bound testosterone by displacing SHBG.

  • Supports cycle performance without suppression

How to Use Proviron

Standard Proviron dosage is 25–50 mg per day, split into two doses. Advanced users may take up to 75–100 mg depending on goals. Proviron is often used during cutting cycles to maintain muscle tone and enhance visual definition when stacked with compounds like:

  • Anavar (Oxandrolone)
  • Testosterone Propionate
  • Sustanon 270

It's also popular in testosterone replacement therapy (TRT) stacks for its libido-boosting and antiestrogenic properties without suppressing endogenous testosterone.

Can Proviron Be Used for PCT?

While Proviron is occasionally included in post-cycle therapy to support libido and mental focus, it is **not a substitute** for proper PCT agents like Clomid or Nolvadex. It does not restore natural LH or FSH production but may enhance well-being during the recovery phase.

Side Effects & Warnings

Proviron is well-tolerated by men and does not aromatize or suppress the hypothalamic-pituitary-gonadal axis (HPG axis). However, possible side effects include:

  • Oily skin or acne (due to DHT activity)
  • Mild hair shedding in DHT-sensitive individuals
  • Not suitable for women – virilization risk

To protect liver function and maintain hormonal health, Proviron should be cycled responsibly and combined with a liver protection supplement if part of an intensive oral steroid protocol.
