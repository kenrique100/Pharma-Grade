What Is Superdrol?

Known for its extreme anabolic potency, Superdrol helps users gain dry, lean muscle mass without significant water retention. It was once sold as a "designer steroid" before becoming classified as a controlled substance.

Side Effects of Superdrol

Superdrol is a potent compound and should be used with respect. Known risks include:

While Superdrol does not aromatize, its strength makes it unsuitable for women and beginners. Bloodwork before and after cycles is strongly recommended.
