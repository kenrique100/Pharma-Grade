Axiolabs T3 25 mcg Tablets

T3 25 mcg contains Liothyronine Sodium, a synthetic form of the active thyroid hormone triiodothyronine (T3). This compound is widely used by bodybuilders, athletes, and fitness enthusiasts to increase metabolic rate, accelerate fat loss, and enhance overall energy expenditure during cutting and recomposition phases.

T3 plays a direct role in regulating metabolism at the cellular level. Unlike T4 (thyroxine), which must be converted into T3 in the body, Liothyronine is already in its active form. This allows Axiolabs T3 to exert immediate metabolic effects without relying on enzymatic conversion.

By increasing basal metabolic rate, T3 promotes greater calorie expenditure, enhanced fat oxidation, and improved nutrient partitioning. For advanced users, it is a powerful tool for achieving a leaner physique when diet and training are already optimized.

The physiological role and pharmacology of Liothyronine Sodium are well documented. A detailed medical overview is available via the National Library of Medicine (PubMed).

Effects

The primary effect of T3 is an increase in metabolic rate. Users typically experience enhanced fat loss, improved calorie utilization, and a noticeable increase in daily energy expenditure.

T3 also increases thermogenesis, which can contribute to faster reductions in body fat when paired with a controlled diet. Many athletes report improved conditioning and a leaner appearance during cutting phases.

When used responsibly, T3 can help preserve training intensity during calorie-restricted phases by supporting energy levels and metabolic efficiency.

Dosage

Typical T3 dosages range from 12.5 mcg to 75 mcg per day, depending on experience level and individual tolerance. Beginners often start with 12.5–25 mcg daily and increase gradually if needed.

Due to its potency, T3 should always be introduced slowly and tapered down at the end of use to allow natural thyroid function to normalize.

Excessive dosing can suppress endogenous thyroid production, so conservative dosing and structured protocols are strongly recommended.

Cycle

T3 is most commonly used during cutting or recomposition cycles. It pairs well with thermogenic agents such as Clenbutaplex, where metabolic output and fat loss are primary goals.

During lean mass preservation phases, T3 is often combined with compounds like Primoplex 100 to help maintain muscle while calories are reduced.

Some advanced users also include T3 alongside anabolic bases such as Testaplex E 250, using thyroid support to enhance nutrient utilization and conditioning.

T3 is not intended for continuous long-term use and should be cycled responsibly with proper tapering.

Possible Side Effects

When used responsibly, T3 is predictable and effective. However, excessive dosing may lead to symptoms associated with hyperthyroidism.

Potential side effects include increased heart rate, sweating, anxiety, insomnia, and muscle loss if calories and protein intake are insufficient.

These effects are dose-dependent and typically resolve once dosage is reduced or discontinued. Proper diet, dosing, and cycle length are essential for safe use.
