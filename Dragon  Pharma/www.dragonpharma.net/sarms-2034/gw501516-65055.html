Buy GW501516 (Cardarine) by Dragon Pharma – Endurance & Fat-Loss Support

GW501516, also known as Cardarine, is a PPARδ (Peroxisome Proliferator-Activated Receptor Delta) agonist developed to support endurance, metabolism, and fat oxidation. Unlike steroids or SARMs, it's non-hormonal, making it a popular choice among athletes and bodybuilders during cutting phases or performance prep.

What is GW501516 (Cardarine) Used For?

Cardarine boosts the body's ability to burn fat as fuel, enhances stamina, and supports metabolic function—without suppressing testosterone. It's often included in cycles focused on definition, recomposition, or cardiovascular performance. GW501516 is not classified as a SARM but is frequently stacked with SARMs like Ostarine (MK-2866) or LGD-4033 for synergistic results.

GW501516 Benefits

  • Enhanced endurance and cardiovascular capacity
  • Promotes fat burning without muscle loss
  • Non-hormonal (no suppression)
  • Ideal for cutting cycles or athletic performance

GW501516 is typically dosed at 10–20 mg per day for 8–12 weeks. Its long half-life allows for once-daily dosing. No PCT is generally required, though some users include natural test boosters during recovery periods for added balance.

Is PCT Required?

Because GW501516 does not affect testosterone levels, a traditional PCT is not mandatory. However, athletes may benefit from general recovery supplements or light PCT agents if stacked with hormonal products.

Frequently Asked Questions About GW501516

Is GW501516 a SARM?

No, it's a PPARδ agonist. However, it's often grouped with SARMs due to similar use cases in cutting and endurance stacks.

Do I need PCT after using Cardarine?

Generally no, as GW501516 is non-hormonal and doesn't suppress testosterone. But you can use light PCT if stacking with SARMs or orals.

What are the benefits of Cardarine?

Increased stamina, fat burning, improved cholesterol profile, and preserved muscle mass during calorie deficits.

How should I take GW501516?

Take 10–20 mg daily for 8–12 weeks. Start low to assess tolerance. Best taken in the morning or before training.

Warnings & Usage Disclaimer

Do not exceed the recommended dosage. Although GW501516 is well-tolerated, long-term human data is limited. Always consult with a healthcare professional before use.
