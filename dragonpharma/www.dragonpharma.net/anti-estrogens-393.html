Post-cycle therapy (PCT) is essential after any anabolic steroid cycle. It helps your body recover, restores natural testosterone production, and prevents unwanted estrogen-related side effects like gynecomastia, water retention, and mood swings.

Why Use Anti-Estrogens After a Steroid Cycle?

During a steroid cycle, your body's hormone levels become imbalanced. Estrogen levels may rise, while natural testosterone production often shuts down. Without proper PCT, users risk losing their gains, experiencing hormonal crashes, and facing long-term side effects.

Anti-estrogens like Arimidex (Anastrozole), Clomid (Clomiphene), and Nolvadex (Tamoxifen) are designed to:

  • Block or reduce estrogen production
  • Stimulate natural testosterone recovery
  • Preserve lean muscle and strength gains
  • Minimize post-cycle side effects
