What is Testoxyl Cypionate 250?

Testoxyl Cypionate 250 by Kalpa Pharmaceuticals is an injectable anabolic steroid containing Testosterone Cypionate dosed at 250 mg/mL. It is a slow-acting ester used primarily in long ester testosterone cycles for muscle growth, hormone replacement therapy (TRT), and strength enhancement.

Key Benefits

  • Promotes steady testosterone levels for 10–14 days
  • Boosts lean muscle mass, recovery, and energy
  • Supports libido, mood, and metabolic function
  • Ideal for TRT or bulking cycles

Usage and Stacking

The typical dosage is 250–500 mg per week for bodybuilding purposes. It is commonly stacked with compounds like Nandroxyl 250 or Dianoxyl 10 for bulking, or with Stanoxyl during cutting cycles to maintain muscle while reducing fat.

Side Effects & Considerations

As an aromatizable compound, Testosterone Cypionate may cause water retention, gynecomastia, or elevated blood pressure. Aromatase inhibitors such as Arimixyl are often used to manage estrogenic side effects. It is not liver toxic, but cholesterol and hematocrit levels should be monitored during use.

Post-Cycle Therapy

PCT is highly recommended after completing a cycle with Testoxyl Cypionate. Agents like Clomixyl or Nolvaxyl are commonly used to help restore endogenous testosterone production.
