What Is HCG (Human Chorionic Gonadotropin)?

HCG mimics the action of luteinizing hormone (LH), sending a signal to the testes to produce testosterone. It is highly effective during anabolic steroid cycles to maintain testicular function, and in post-cycle therapy (PCT) to jumpstart endogenous testosterone recovery.
