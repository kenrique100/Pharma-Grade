GHRP-2 (Growth Hormone Releasing Peptide-2) is a synthetic hexapeptide used in experimental models to trigger growth hormone release via ghrelin receptor activation. The 5 mg vial by Peptide Hubs offers researchers a potent, lab-grade tool to study GH elevation, recovery, metabolism, and hormonal feedback loops. How GHRP-2 Works

GHRP-2 binds to the GHS-R1a receptor in the pituitary and hypothalamus, leading to a sharp increase in pulsatile GH release. This action mimics the effect of endogenous ghrelin, though with greater specificity and potency. Unlike GHRP-6, GHRP-2 has a lower appetite-stimulating effect, making it suitable for studies focused on lean mass maintenance, IGF-1 upregulation, and metabolic enhancement.
