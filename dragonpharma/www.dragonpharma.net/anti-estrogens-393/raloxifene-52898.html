What is Raloxifene Used For?

Originally developed to treat osteoporosis in postmenopausal women, Raloxifene gained popularity in the bodybuilding world for its ability to bind selectively to estrogen receptors. This action helps reduce the impact of estrogen on breast tissue, making it highly effective in preventing and reversing gynecomastia during steroid cycles.
