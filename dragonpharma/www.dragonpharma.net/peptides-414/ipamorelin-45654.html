What is Ipamorelin?

Ipamorelin is a selective growth hormone releasing peptide (GHRP) developed to mimic the natural hormone ghrelin. Unlike other peptides in its class, Ipamorelin provides GH stimulation with minimal impact on cortisol or prolactin levels—making it one of the most user-friendly peptides for muscle growth and anti-aging protocols.
