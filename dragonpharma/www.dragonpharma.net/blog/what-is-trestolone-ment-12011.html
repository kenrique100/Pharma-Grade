Trestolone Acetate (MENT): The Most Powerful Bulking Compound You’ve Never Tried

What Is Trestolone Acetate?

Trestolone Acetate, often referred to simply as MENT, is one of the most potent anabolic steroids ever developed. Originally researched as a potential male contraceptive due to its powerful hormonal effects, MENT has since become a favorite among experienced bodybuilders seeking extreme mass gains in a short period.

Unlike testosterone, MENT does not require conversion to dihydrotestosterone (DHT) to become active. Its high anabolic index and minimal interaction with sex hormone-binding globulin (SHBG) allow it to exert powerful effects on muscle tissue — fast.

Why MENT Is So Effective for Bulking

MENT stands out for one reason: raw power. Its anabolic-to-androgenic ratio is significantly higher than testosterone, and many users report feeling "on" within just days of the first dose.

Here's why it's considered elite:

  • Rapid size and strength gains
  • Highly bioavailable in its acetate form
  • Minimal binding to SHBG → more free steroid in the system
  • Doesn't convert to DHT → reduced DHT-related side effects
  • Promotes high nitrogen retention and protein synthesis

MENT cycles are often described as transformative — delivering dramatic muscle fullness and strength increases even in advanced users.

While testosterone serves as a base steroid in many stacks, MENT is a power-move — best used by seasoned athletes looking to break through plateaus.

Despite its impressive results, MENT isn't without risks:
